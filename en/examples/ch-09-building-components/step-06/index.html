Accessible Modal

Building an accessible modal properly from scratch is a month of work. Radix Dialog gives you focus trap, escape to close, body scroll lock, portal, full ARIA dialog, focus return to the trigger. Your job is to style and wire the state.

Three ways to close: X button, Escape key, click on the overlay. Tab navigates only inside the modal (focus trap). When you close, focus returns to the button that opened it.
